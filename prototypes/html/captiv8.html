Assay description

The CAPTIV-8 score is computed by combining the following attributes: presence of a cancer driving virus (VIRUSBreakend 2.13.2), loss of function SWISNF variants (MuTect2 GATK 4.2.6.1), Genomic Tumour Mutation Burden (MuTect2 GATK 4.2.6.1), Transcripts per million (TPM) values (RSEM 1.3.3), Estimate of immune cell types (CIBERSORT immunedeconv 2.1.0), additional points are included if sample is a lymph node biopsy or a colorectal cancer (Requisition system).
